Discarded Possessions - white cloth, household items. Site-specific installation. Georgetown, Penang, Malaysia.

Benedict Mitchell, Wesley Pan, Lily Xu, Mikala Tavener-Hanks, Bella Payne.

This studio took place as part of the New Colombo Plan scholarship program and was led by Sean Cordeiro.

More information can be found on this blog post by Mikala-Tavener-Hanks.
